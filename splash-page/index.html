Our features

What sets us apart

Having witnessed first hand the evolution of the Chicago rave scene, we have a sincere appreciation for the history of this culture, but we know the times and they are a-changin'. With the rapid dissemination of technology throughout all aspects of our lives comes a heightened need to get creative and do something different. So... In addition to offering lower service charges for online transactions, and a more efficient and secure process at the door, we're also building a fan-fueled event curation tool.

Crowdsourced event curation

A simple user-focused interface allows us to ask you, the fans, the BIG questions. Who do you want to perform? What overall production theme do you want to see? Where do you want the event to be held?

Empowers the community

Using your answers, we host special pop-up events feauturing the most popular selections from those big questions. It's like a communal choose-your-own-adventure book, but it's not a book. It's a rave!

Rewarding fans for coming out

First, we offer reduced service charges when you buy tickets for your favorite shows. When it comes to helping us curate the events we produce, every ticket you've purchased increases the power of your voice.

Gamifies local nightlife

A bit of healthy competition can be a wonderful thing. Now concert goers have a real say in what happens in their city, and even better, fans that attend shows often, know that their opinion matters the most.

Managed ticket ownership

We're leveredging the latest trends in cryptographically-secured digital assets to make fake tickets a thing of the past. This all happens automatically without any change to your normal at-the-door experience.

Eliminates ticket scalping

The Blox Office™ digital ticket is only transferable through our platform. This eliminates the secondary market - protecting customers and ensuring prices for events remain where they started.
